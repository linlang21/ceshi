Level 1 – Polymorphic string obfuscation

I'd be careful relying on 'Hiding from PEB'

I believe the game doesn't care about the PEB(so far it suggested that). Instead, it looks at the VAD Tree. If you have a memory page marked as executable (either RX or RWX) that isn't backed by a valid file on disk, you are basically lighting yourself on fire to the driver, regardless of whether you unlinked from the PEB.
